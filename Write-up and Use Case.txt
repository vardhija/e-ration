Objective:
-> To digitalize the process of ration distribution of food items in the State.

Use Cases:
There will be two users:
1. Government (with Admin privillages)
2. Citizen 

The Government
---------------
The Government can:
* Approve citizen applications (user creation request).
* Create items with their details.
* Allot items to citizens.
* See the citizens orders.
* Sent notifications to the citizens.

The Citizen
---------------
The citizens can:
* See the government notifications
* Select their needed stuffs from the ration allotted to them.
* Check out the items
